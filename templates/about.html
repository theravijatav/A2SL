We are a team of five final-year students from MITRC College, passionate about leveraging technology to create meaningful solutions. Our project focuses on developing an innovative tool that converts speech and text into sign language animations using Blender 3D, aimed at aiding individuals with communication difficulties. With diverse skill sets in programming, design, and research, we collaborated intensively to ensure our project reflects our commitment and creativity. We dedicated ourselves to this initiative, striving for excellence and inclusivity, and we believe our work can make a significant impact on the lives of specially-abled individuals.
